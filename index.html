Spacecraft Control Engineer & Astrodynamicist

About Me

Passionate and experienced spacecraft control engineer and astrodynamicist. My work focuses on spacecraft parameter estimation, attitude dynamics, and control. I have been part of a CubeSat mission, contributed to lunar mission planning, and developed advanced estimation and control algorithms for satellite systems.

Projects

Reentry Parameter Estimation Simulator

Designed a testbed simulator for estimating lift-to-drag ratio and atmospheric density during Mars reentry using extended Kalman filtering on IMU and pressure sensor data.

Autonomous Attitude Control Compensation

Implemented a MATLAB algorithm that repositions spacecraft testbed balance masses in real-time to offset gravity, enabling high-accuracy satellite orientation testing.

Lunar Orbital Mission Planning

Performed orbital trajectory optimization and remote sensing targeting for a lunar exploration mission using GMAT and custom orbital mechanics scripts.
